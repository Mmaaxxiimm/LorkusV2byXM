Do you want to change the mind-set of your team? Or do you wish to prepare your organization for change? Don’t look any further, b2b is your partner!

How b2b can help

b2b (better2best) provides trainings, workshops and lectures inspiring your team, your company or your audience to do better every day so that they can become the best in the long run.

How to show more leadership as an individual? How to improve collaboration? How to be ready as a team for the big change? I’d love to tell you. You’ve come to the right address. Interested in a training or lecture? Well, just continue reading…

We offer 13 modules devided into 3 themes (the individual, the team and the organisation). Modules can also be choosen a la carte. We will always make sure the combination makes an interesting story. This story is brought to you either as a training (including workshop on ‘how to deal with the learnings in practive’) or as a lecture/keynote.

Easy to understand, ready to use

The concepts that are covered in each of the modules are easy to understand, practical and ready to use, whatever you position, whatever your sector. Moreover, the new insights are not only applicable at work but are also of use in your private life.

You should plan 30’ to 90’ per module. Each of the modules can be extended with a workshop on “how to deal with the learnings in practice”. Add 15’ to 30’ per workshop.

We first look into the mirror and discover the modules that are directly linked with the individual

We ask ourselves the following questions: What makes me tick? Why am I who I am? Why do I behave in a certain way? Why do I have my own outlook on things? What are my habits? How do I discover new insights?

Mood – Why did everything work out yesterday, but no longer today? Are you aware of the impact of your mood?

Energy – How does one arouse that spark of team-spirit? How to create positive energy among others? Did you know that the key is your own energy level?

Win-win – Does someone else always have to lose so that you can win? Not all the time. What other alternatives are there?

Filters – Why does my colleague see things differently? Maybe he or she creates her own reality? Or maybe it’s you?

Focus – Why aren’t you as effective or creative as you would like to be? Perhaps it’s because you get easily distracted?

We discover the modules that focus on the interaction between two individuals.

During this journey we engage to interact and discover what type of behaviour is helpful. We explore our surroundings and are open to new encounters.

Curiosity - How to adopt a curious attitude? Did you know that curiosity is the key to new innovative thinking and ideas?

Feedback – People want feedback about how they are doing and what they can improve. But how do you handle that? How to best motivate others?

Role model – How do we influence the people that surround us? Are you modelling the behaviour you promote? And does this translate itself in your actions?

Listening – You think you are a good listener? Perhaps, some of your co-workers say otherwise?

We discover the modules that facilitate collaboration in a team and that can be applied in whatever organization, company or association.

Collaboration – How do you encourage teamwork? How do you make sure that your team works in the flow and performs optimally?

Behaviour styles – How to adapt to your interlocutor? Are you speaking the language that suits your partner?

Accountability – How can you make the difference? How to initiate and encourage empowerment?

Priorities – Are you spending your time well? Do you take time to decide what is really important?
